Copenhagen

Copenhagen is a Summit in which developed and developing countries meet on climate change.

All the countries gathered to find a solution so as to save the climatic changes so that we all can have a healthy environment for our coming generations.

India and China are trying to harmonize the positions of their countries on climate change. Premier Wen Jiabao called up Prime Minister Manmohan Singh to work together at the Copenhagen conference to present a more unified picture against the developed countries taking a position that could push these two countries into taking legally binding emission cuts.
